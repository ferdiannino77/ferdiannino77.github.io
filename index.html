About Me

I'm a Statistics undergraduate at the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) Surabaya, currently maintaining a GPA of 3.58/4.00 on a Cum Laude track.

My academic journey centers on applying statistical methods to real-world challenges — from building social piety indices for local governments, to producing national-award-winning scientific papers on community resilience and digital approaches to disaster mitigation.

Beyond the classroom, I lead the Scientific Department of HIMASTA ITS, mentor junior researchers, and serve as a Master of Ceremony at various academic and institutional events. I believe data is most powerful when it tells a story that drives change.
